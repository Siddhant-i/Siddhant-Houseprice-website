🧠 How to Use the Logical Thinking Suggestion Box

We value your creative ideas! Follow the simple steps below to share your insights with us and help improve the House Price Prediction experience for everyone.

1

Click the Button

On the homepage, click the "💡 Suggest an Idea" button to open the dialog box.

2

Enter Your Details

Fill in your name, email, and write your idea or suggestion clearly.

3

Submit Your Idea

Press the “Submit” button. You'll see a confirmation message.

4

Done! 🎉

Your suggestion is now on its way to our team. Thank you for contributing!

💬 Note: All suggestions are reviewed by our team. We may contact you for more details if needed.
